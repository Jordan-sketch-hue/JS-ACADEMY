Production standards · All reels
On-screen text
Bold white text (dark reels) or #141414 (light reel). Appear in sync with VO — hook lines only, not full sentences. Fade in, no bounce. Bottom-third placement so it doesn't compete with TikTok chrome.
Music layer
No AI sounds. Royalty-free ambient track under VO at 15–20% volume. TikTok sound library: "calm ambient business" or "cinematic minimal." VO must lead clearly — never compete.
Brand colour rule
JST reels: white surface, #141414 ink, zero color. JSM reels: #141414 surface, white ink, zero color. No blue, no amber, no gradients — mono only. Color = violation of both brand specs.
Editing style
Cut on beat or on key VO phrases. Pace: deliberate, premium — not fast-cut hyper edits. JST: clean, minimal, spacious. JSM: cinematic, slightly slower, luxury feel. Each reel = 3–6 total cuts maximum.
CTA frame
Final 3–4 seconds = static or near-static CTA card. JST: white bg, #141414 text, pill button. JSM: #141414 bg, white text, clean. "Link in bio" always the call. No animated arrows or emojis in frame.
Azure SSML tip
Use <break time="500ms"/> for the pauses noted in scripts. Wrap the hook phrase in <emphasis level="moderate">. Export at 44.1kHz WAV, then compress to AAC for TikTok.
