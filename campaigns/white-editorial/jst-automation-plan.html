The Product

What JST Automation Actually Is

Most Caribbean businesses are running on WhatsApp messages, paper notebooks, and manual spreadsheets. They already use tools like Square, Shopify, Google Forms, or Calendly — they just don't know those tools can talk to each other. JST connects them, builds the automations, and maintains them monthly. The client touches nothing technical.

The pitch in one sentence: "You already have the tools. We connect them so your orders, bookings, emails, invoices, and follow-ups run automatically — without you lifting a finger."

Example for a restaurant: Customer fills out a Google Form order → WhatsApp confirmation fires instantly → order goes to a Google Sheet → Square invoice is created → follow-up message sent 3 days later asking for a review. JST builds all of this. Client pays $199/month to keep it running and updated.

Example for a real estate agent: Facebook Lead Ad fires → lead is added to Google Sheets CRM → WhatsApp message sent within 60 seconds → DocuSign agreement triggered when they respond → reminder sent if no reply in 48 hours.

Sales Process

The 3-Move Close — From Stranger to Signed Client

Every sale follows the same sequence. Do not skip steps. The free audit is the door.

1
The Observation Message
Open with something specific about THEIR business — not a generic pitch. Shows you did the work.
WhatsApp / DM — Message 1
"Hey [Name], I came across [Business Name] on Instagram. Love what you're doing with [specific thing]. Quick question — are you still taking orders through DM? I noticed you might be losing a few because of the back-and-forth. We just set up an automated order system for a similar business here in [location] and they cut their response time to under 2 minutes. Would it be worth a 20-minute call to see if we could do the same for you? No pitch — just a walkthrough of what's possible."
2
The Free Audit Call
On the call, ask 5 questions. Don't pitch — diagnose. Then show 3 specific automations you could build for them.
Audit Call — 5 Questions to Ask
1. "Walk me through what happens when a new customer contacts you — step by step."
2. "Which part of that takes you the most time?"
3. "What tools do you currently use — WhatsApp, Square, Google Forms, anything like that?"
4. "How many leads or orders would you say you lose because of delayed follow-up?"
5. "If we could automate one thing starting Monday, what would hurt the least to lose from your plate?"

Then show them a live demo of a relevant automation (pre-built in Make.com). Let them see it running. That's the close.
3
The Scoped Proposal (same day)
Send within 2 hours of the call. One page. Three things you'll build. Fixed price. 14-day delivery.
Proposal Message
"Hey [Name], great speaking with you. Based on our call, here's exactly what we'd build for [Business Name]:

✦ [Automation 1 — specific to their business]
✦ [Automation 2]
✦ [Automation 3]

Price: $[X]/month. Setup delivered in 14 days. No contract — cancel anytime after month 3.

To get started I need a 50% deposit ($[X/2]) and 30 minutes with you this week to connect your tools. Ready to go?"
