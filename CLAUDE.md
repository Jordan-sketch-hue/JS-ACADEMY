## Ad Campaigns (Psychology-Based)
### JST (5 campaigns)
1. **Loss Aversion** — "Every week without a website, someone else gets your customer"
2. **Price Anchoring** — Show J$180K app first → J$55K site looks like a deal
3. **Decoy Pricing** — Basic J$25K / Pro J$55K ← push this / Enterprise J$120K
4. **Scarcity** — "We take 3 new clients per month. 1 spot left."
5. **Social Proof** — "Ferguson Law went from zero to booked online. 4 weeks."

### JSM (3 campaigns)
1. **Loss Aversion** — "You're posting every day and getting nothing back. That's not a content problem."
2. **Urgency + Scarcity** — "Q3 retainer spots close July 31."
3. **Value Reframe** — "Our clients generate J$300K+/mo from content we manage for J$55K."

1. **Outcome** — Results first, never services ("Ferguson Law: zero to booked online, 4 weeks")
